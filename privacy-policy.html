Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

Welcome to Pi Arena Elite. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it within the Pi Network ecosystem. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you access our application via the Pi Browser, we collect the following limited information securely through the official Pi SDK:

  • Pi Username: To identify you on the leaderboard and save your purchased in-game skins.
  • Transaction Data: Payment Identifiers (Payment IDs and TXIDs) related to your in-game purchases on the Pi Blockchain.

2. How We Use Your Information

We strictly use this data to provide the gaming service:

  • To authenticate your identity via the Pi Network API.
  • To verify and complete your Pi cryptocurrency payments for match entries and skins.
  • To update global high scores accurately.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not have access to your Pi wallet passphrase, personal email, phone number, or physical location. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or sell your data to advertisers.

4. Data Security

Your data is processed securely. All cryptocurrency transactions are handled externally by the Pi Wallet and Pi Blockchain. We only store the confirmation receipts.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically to comply with Pi Network Developer terms. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.
